    Projects

    Project-1:

    AMIGO-ASSIST


    The whole theme of our work is to provide assistance to the vehicles which had broken down in an unknown location. The project is to create a web application that should be user friendly so that user’s can easily access our web page. Based upon the location of the user the nearby mechanic shops will be displayed for them, then the users select any of our partnered mechanic shops. In case,If user’s vehicle had exhausted without oil, our team will provide the basic requirement of 1 litre so that they can reach nearby filling station. In case of any emergency user’s can appeal to the emergency lines or patrolling department, for seeking assistance.


    Project-2:

    LEVY A TAX ON


    Motto of our project is to provide transparency in the transactions that will be made by government, we have 3 major leverages to the existing systems. Corruption is the main culprit of India’s slowed development. To avoid this we make the transactions made by the government very transparent and clear to the citizens by using the distributed ledger of the block chain. The process of paying tax is a time consuming one, the payer should calculate and himself should pay it, to avoid this we use a blockchain platform called Ethereum, where we use smart contracts for automating the process of tax calculation and the contract will automatically pay the amount. In the process of auditing the organizational data we have to appoint a specific personal for it and he will charge some amount,to avoid this we use a ledger of blockchain to generate the report of transactional data, that makes the auditing process simple.

    Project-3:

    Weather Reporting System


    The project’s main theme is to predict the surrounding weather by using sensors and to create an IoT component which will report the weather to connected device. Here, we use rain, light, humidity sensors to predict the surrounding weather conditions. These sensing components will send the information to the connected device like mobiles.laptops etc… Based on that information we can easily predict the weather conditions outside. All these components collectively called as IoT device.
